INTRODUCTION

The following visualizations are based upon a data set that deals with Premier League data which is basically a Soccer tournament played in England. The data related to 12 seasons is available. In this data story, we try to analyze all the data related to various seasons and try to put forth some interesting aspects about the data. Some interesting questions followed by a visualization that answers the question and a brief para about the visualization can been seen in the following data story.

Visualization - I

What are the top 5 seasons in which most away goals were scored and all the teams that were associated with those seasons with more than 50 away goals?

WHAT IS THE ANSWER:
There are 5 seasons in which most number of away goals were scored. The seasons are 2011-2012, 2012-2013, 2013-2014, 2014-2015 and 2015-2016.

HOW DOES THE VISUALIZATION HELP:
It is clearly depicted that there are 5 differnt seasons marked by 5 different colors where the most number of away goals were socred. This visualization also helps us in identifying the teams that were associated with those seasons and who have scored a minimum of 50 away goals.


Visualization - II

What are the top teams that averaged more than 80 Goals per Season?

WHAT IS THE ANSWER:
The are 5 teams that averaged more than 80 goals in various seasons. They are Chelsea, Liverpool, Manchester City, Manchester United and Tootenham Hotspur.

HOW DOES THE VISUALIZATION HELP:
It is clearly depicted there are 5 teams that have scored more then 80 goals in various seasons. The visualization helps in identifying and comparing the top teams that fall under this criteria. It can also be seen that the teams that have scored a significant amout of away goals (from Visualization I) fall under this category.


Visualization - III

How do various statistics like away goals, own goals and corner kicks relate to each other for the Teams mentioned in the above visualization?

WHAT IS THE ANSWER:
The statistics like average of Away Goals, Corner Kicks taken and Own Goals are depicted and how they are related to each other can be seen. The seasons in which the average of away goals was atleast 1 per match are show.

HOW DOES THE VISUALIZATION HELP:
The visualization helps us in understanding the answer by clearly depicting the statistics like Average of Away goals, Corner kicks taken and Oome Goals. It can be seen that there is a significant influence of the avereage of Own goals and the corner kicks taken on the Average of away goals scored by the teams.


Visualization - IV

What are the factors that influenced a particular team to not win a match and eventually the season?

WHAT IS THE ANSWER:
The penalty saves and the total red cards in a season was a prime factor for a team for their average win percentage.

HOW DOES THE VISUALIZATION HELP:
The visualization helps us in understanding the answer by clearly depicting how the average amount of red cards and the penalty saves influence the win percentage for a particular team. In brief, by taking into account the above visualizations, we can say that there are a lot factors that influence a team to win. Just by scoring a lot of Home goals is not sufficient if the amount of away goals and the penalties given are considerably high.
